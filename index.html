SPA SERVICES

Plutobeautycafe is an award winning concept, based on making day spas accessible and affordable for the whole community. We work hard to create a friendly day spa environment that is inclusive and welcoming, providing affordable luxury delivered by friendly, trained professionals. You can always expect a warm welcome when you visit our spas. Our staff are chosen not only for their technical expertise but also their positive attitudes and friendly characters. If you’re a first time visitor to a day spa we’ll be delighted to give you a tour and showcase the fantastic benefits that thermal, well-being and beauty treatments can bring you.
